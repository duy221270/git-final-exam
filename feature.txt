--- Mo ta tinh nang A: Tim kiem san pham ---
1. Them mot thanh tim kiem o dau trang chu.
2. Nguoi dung co the nhap ten san pham de tim kiem.
3. He thong se goi y cac ket qua phu hop khi nguoi dung dang go.
4. Ket qua tim kiem se duoc hien thi o mot trang rieng.
5. Trang ket qua se co chuc nang loc theo gia, danh muc san pham.
